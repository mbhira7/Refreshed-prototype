About Rise

The Rise Modular Alarm Clock is designed to make the process of waking up more comfortable, while being customizable to the customerâ€™s needs. Choose between several modules (sold separately) to connect to the clock to tailor the morning experience just for you.

The interface

The primary window consists of a digital clock in the center with a date and temperature above. Below are two digital buttons that take you through either a screen for settings or for alarms. The alarm screen, shows when the alarm goes off. It retains the same idea from the primary screen, but the buttons are replaced with two more prominent buttons that allows the owner of the alarm to either stop it, or snooze to add a few extra minutes before the alarm would go off again.
