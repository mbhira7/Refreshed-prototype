Our vision and team

We are a small but passionate team of 5 individuals. We first met through university, and shared the common problem of having difficulties with waking up in the morning. As we were brainstorming solutions to this problem, we found we all had different preferences for how we would like to wake up. Our product concept started becoming bloated with features that only some of us found useful. This sparked the idea that would become one of the most important parts of our company; The idea that you only pay for the features you want to use. It is our belief that this is the most effective way to satisfy our customers' needs.
